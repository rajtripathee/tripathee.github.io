About

Background &

I'm an application and systems engineer in the semiconductor industry, focused on helping customers design, integrate, and optimize solutions built around complex semiconductor products. My work sits at the intersection of deep technical knowledge and practical problem-solving — turning spec sheets into working systems.

Whether it's supporting evaluation and bring-up, debugging system-level issues, or collaborating with design teams to find the right fit for an application, I enjoy the challenge of making things work in the real world — not just on paper.

When I'm not at a bench or on a customer call, you'll find me on a trail somewhere in the mountains or in my workshop building something from raw lumber. Both pursuits share the same satisfaction: taking raw material, applying craft and patience, and ending up with something that holds together.
